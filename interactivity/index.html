Interactive works with augmented reality

My interactive art - works are listed here. Most of theme are paintings with augmented reality, go to my artworks page to discover more.

I work with open source projects like AR.js, Three.js and other. All the app are based on Html and Javascript programming languages. All you need is a smartphone or a tablet and... the Artwork! Yes you can not do so much with them. But i plan to realize some applications that you can download and print an artwork and interact with it! Stay tuned!
